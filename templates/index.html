Build your confidence for phobia!


A phobia is a type of anxiety disorder. Phobias are among the most common of all mental illnesses, and they are usually the most successfully treated.

Face your fears

Avoiding fears only makes them scarier. Whatever your fear, if you face it, it should start to fade. If you panic one day getting into a lift, for example, it's best to get back into a lift the next day.

Don't try to be perfect

Life is full of stresses, yet many of us feel that our lives must be perfect. Bad days and setbacks will always happen, and it's important to remember that life is messy.

Reward yourself

Finally, give yourself a treat. When you've made that call you've been dreading, for example, reinforce your success by treating yourself to a massage, a country walk, a meal out, a book, a DVD, or whatever little gift makes you happy.

About Us

History

This project was made in a Singapore hackathon named, Mindful Hack. In this in-person hackathon, student were required to make an application to help lift the mental health blocks.

Professional understanding-mental-health talks were given and it was a calling for the people at the hackathon to create something that is meaningful.

Mission & Vission

This project is cater to people who may want to detect themselves for phobia and a self-trainer at their comfort. Hopefully, more people are willing to train themselves and improve their mental health over time. Currently only Scopophobia is added.

It steps away from the traditional thoughts of online mental health platforms. There is no therapist coaches or counselor. The team believes that everyone needs to be self-directed in training one self's mental health.
